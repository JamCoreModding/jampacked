## ❓ FAQ

- **Are performance mods included?** Yes, we have included standard performance mods (such as Sodium and ModernFix).
- **Are shaders included?** Iris is included, along with some of our shaderpack suggestions - but feel free to use your own.
- **Why 1.21.1 NeoForge?** We felt that it had the best selection of mods as of when Jampacked was started. There will likely be future iterations of Jampacked that support different mod loaders and Minecraft versions.
